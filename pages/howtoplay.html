*️⃣ Learn The Game.

You're what is called a Shooter and you're always in place of Rolling the Dice. On your first Roll Two win the game, you will need to Roll a 7 or 11. To lose the game on your first roll if you roll a 2,3 or 12 then you lose the game. If on your first Roll you roll any of the remaining possible numbers like 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, or 10 this will be your Pointer. The Pointer moves on to another Roll you will need to Roll the same number as your pointer to Win the game but if you Roll a 7 then you lose the Game. You will need to keep rolling until you either Roll your Pointer Or the 7.
